Is Ross Free?

NOT YET

Ross Ulbricht is currently serving a double life sentence plus forty years without the possibility of parole, for non-violent crimes.

He has already spent more than a decade in prison. Friends, family, and allies have long been advocating for his freedom.

In 2024 during his election campaign, President Trump promised to commute his sentence:

>

“If you vote for me,” he promised, “on Day 1, I will commute the sentence of Ross Ulbricht to a sentence of time served. He's already served 11 years. We're going to get him home.”
